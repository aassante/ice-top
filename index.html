Innovation in Thermotherapy

Thermopraxis Holdings, Inc. (“THI” or “Thermopraxis”) is headquartered in New York City and has two strategic subsidiaries, one based in New York City and one based in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.

Thermopraxis was founded by Renato Rozental, MD, PhD and Anthony L. Finley, LLB to develop and commercialize innovative medical devices that provide emergency hypothermic therapy to reduce the effects of traumatic brain injury (“TBI”) after a head injury.

Our Product





>THI’s principal product, Thermocrown, consists of a flexible and inflatable multi-layer hypothermia cover for the head and neck regions and a cartridge with compressed cooling gas. Both Thermocrown and the cartridge are single-use.

Early initiation of hypothermia within the Golden Hour of acute care is essential to provide prophylactic treatment to limit the progression of injury.

Thermocrown is the first practical EMS hypothermia device specific for the treatment of TBI and is designed to move hypothermia treatment for TBI from the in-hospital to the pre-hospital or acute care setting. The cooling rate is controlled and moderate to maximize the safety profile and to prevent side effects.

Thermocrown is effective, safe, portable, affordable in large quantities, and suitable for use globally without modification.

The IP is secured in major domestic and international markets.
